What is your favorite color?

January 2019, Color Preferences, First Post

Our preference for certain colors is one way we are different from each other. Babies start showing this preference as early as 12 weeks old. Pyschologists, designers, and marketers world-wide study the preferences and associations we have with color to understand more about those they want to impact. Choose your favorite color and see how that compares to what other people prefer.

What is your birthday?

January 2019, Birthday Problem

How many people does there need to be in a room before there will be a pair of people with the same birthday? Surprisingly, with just 23 people there is a 50% chance that there will be pair. At 70 people, the probability increases to 99.9%. This principle in probability theory is called the birthday problem. Generate the heatmap below and find your birthday. What days of the year stick out and why do you think that is?
